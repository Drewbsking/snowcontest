Official Contest Rules

The Road Commission for Oakland County’s “Guess How Much Snow We’ll Get” challenge rewards the closest snowfall prediction for the White Lake Township station’s winter contest window.

How to Enter

  • Submit a single guess (to the nearest tenth of an inch) for total snowfall between December 2 and March 31.
  • Include your full name and district/department.
  • Email or interoffice mail your entry to Leanne DeRousse (Managing Directors’ Office) before 4:00 PM on the published deadline, typically the Monday preceding the contest season.
  • Entries are open to all active RCOC employees; one submission per person.

Scoring & Winners

  • Official Contest: The winning guess is the closest without going over the verified contest total (Dec 2 → Mar 31). If every entry is above the total, the closest overall is recognized but no official prize is awarded.
  • Seasonal Spotlight: We also recognize the closest guess (same scoring rule) for the full snow year (Jul 1 → Jun 30) as an unofficial bragging-rights leader.
  • Ties are allowed—multiple entrants receive recognition when they are equally close under the scoring rules.

Verification & Prize

  • Official totals are taken from the NOAA ACIS White Lake 4E station (SID 208941).
  • Trace amounts (“T”) count as 0.0 in the totals.
  • Official winners earn a half-day off prize (expires August 31 of the following year).

Questions about the contest? Contact the Managing Directors’ Office for clarification.
