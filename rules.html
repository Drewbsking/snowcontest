Official Contest Rules

Traffic Safety – Engineering is competing in the Road Commission for Oakland County’s “Guess How Much Snow We’ll Get” challenge. This contest is for Traffic Safety – Engineering employees only. Entry is free, and the official data source is the White Lake Township station on https://www.rcc-acis.org/home.

How to Enter

  • Use the form on the home page.
  • Participation is limited to Traffic Safety – Engineering employees; one submission per person.
  • Submit a single guess (to one decimal place, for example 50.1) for total seasonal snowfall.
  • Total seasonal snowfall is defined as July 2025 through June 2026.
  • Provide predictions for the five Holiday Spotlight dates by calling whether snowfall occurs on each day (Yes/No only — no snow total guess required).

Scoring & Winners

  • Seasonal Total: Winners are determined by the absolute closest prediction to the verified seasonal total (closest to actual, whether over or under). Guesses are judged to one decimal place.
  • Holiday Spotlight: For each of the five spotlight holidays, entrants make a Yes/No call on whether measurable snow (≥0.1") occurs that day. All entrants who call the outcome correctly split the holiday pot equally.
  • Ties for any seasonal or holiday category share the relevant recognition and prize money.

Verification & Prize

  • Official totals are taken from the NOAA ACIS White Lake 4E station (SID 208941).
  • Trace amounts (“T”) count as 0.0 in the totals.
  • The seasonal champion earns a $35 grand prize.
  • Each Holiday Spotlight has a $10 pot. Entrants must call the snow/no-snow outcome correctly to remain eligible; qualifying winners split the pot evenly.
  • Prize payouts will be made no later than the end of April.

Questions or concerns? Contact the Traffic Safety – Engineering contest organizer.
