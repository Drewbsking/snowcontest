Official Contest Rules

Traffic Safety – Engineering is competing in the Road Commission for Oakland County’s “Guess How Much Snow We’ll Get” challenge. Entry is free, and the official data source is the White Lake Township station on https://www.rcc-acis.org/home.

How to Enter

  • Use the form on the home page.
  • Participation is limited to Traffic Safety – Engineering employees; one submission per person.
  • Submit a single guess (to one decimal place, for example 50.1) for total seasonal snowfall.
  • Total seasonal snowfall is defined as July 2025 through June 2026.
  • Provide predictions for the five holiday spotlight dates, including whether snowfall occurs on each day.

Scoring & Winners

  • Seasonal Total: The “Price Is Right” rule applies—whoever comes closest to the verified seasonal total without going over wins. Guesses are judged to one decimal place.
  • All-Over Scenario: If every seasonal guess exceeds the official total, the overall closest guess is recognized for bragging rights only.
  • Holiday Spotlight: For each of the five spotlight holidays, entrants submit a snowfall total (one decimal place) and a yes/no call on whether it snows that day. The closest total without going over wins the holiday pot; ties split the pot equally.
  • Ties for any seasonal or holiday category share the relevant recognition and prize money.

Verification & Prize

  • Official totals are taken from the NOAA ACIS White Lake 4E station (SID 208941).
  • Trace amounts (“T”) count as 0.0 in the totals.
  • The seasonal champion earns a $35 grand prize.
  • Each holiday spotlight has a $13 pot. Entrants must call the snow/no-snow outcome correctly to remain eligible; qualifying winners split the pot evenly.

Questions about the contest? Contact the Managing Directors’ Office for clarification.
